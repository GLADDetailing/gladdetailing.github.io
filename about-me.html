Meet the Founder

Hello, I'm Miguel Goizueta, a student at the University of Florida's engineering school, majoring in computer science and business administration. My journey into the world of car detailing began in 2022 in my hometown of West Palm Beach, where I discovered my passion for meticulously caring for vehicles. Since then, I've embarked on a relentless pursuit of excellence in both academia and entrepreneurship.

My dedication to mastering the art of car detailing stems from a profound belief in the transformative power of hard work and dedication. Through countless hours of hands-on experience, I've gained invaluable insights into the right products and techniques necessary to deliver exceptional results.

As I navigate the challenges of balancing my studies with my entrepreneurial endeavors, I remain driven by a relentless desire to learn and grow. Every detail I take on is infused with a deep sense of purpose and a commitment to exceeding expectations.

Thank you Gainesville for considering GLAD Detailing. I am immensely grateful for the opportunity to share my passion with you and to embark on this journey together.

As always, my goal is to make sure you're GLAD we detail your car! — Miguel Goizueta
